WHO WE ARE

We are a 40 year old company based in Thane, India and are a primary Dealer, Distributor and Traders of Strong, Reliable, Affordable, Compliant, Seamless and ERW/HSAW/LSAW/HFW pipes and tubes that can be used for Structural, line pipes, Water pipeline, Gas line and absolutely anywhere. We provide the most affordable and end-to-end piping solution, Apart from this, We also provide state of the art and secure Warehousing solutions by providing Warehouses on rent for Storage of Goods and daily operations, ideal for Storage of Agricultural produce, waste paper management, storage of steel and machinery items, etc.

OUR PRODUCTS

MS Seamless and ERW pipes, round pipes ranging from 3" to 80" and Square section pipes from 25 x 25 to 400 x 400 from brands like TATA, RATNAMANI, JINDAL, SURYA, MSL at a very reasonable rate. We provide delivery across India.

Square & Round Pipes

High precision and durable tubes directly sourced from brands like Tata Steel, Ratnamani Metals, Surya Prakash, MSL, Jindal Nashik and Guj. These pipes have great strength and are very durable, can be used in line pipes for water and gas, can be used for structures.
